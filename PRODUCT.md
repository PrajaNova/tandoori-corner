# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Restaurant operators and staff managing Tandoori Corner's menu, bookings, event enquiries, online orders, and site content during service prep or quiet admin windows.

## Product Purpose

Give staff a fast, trustworthy back office for keeping the public restaurant experience accurate and current. Success means common edits are obvious, status queues are visible, and menu updates do not require developer help.

## Brand Personality

Focused, warm, operational.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic spreadsheet dumps, decorative dashboard noise, marketing-page hero sections, and playful controls that slow down repeated admin work.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the next operational action, not explanation.
- Show useful counts and status before tables.
- Keep creation flows structured so staff can move from blank form to publishable item quickly.
- Use restrained brand warmth without reducing data density.
- Make failures and empty states clear enough to recover without guessing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
